Welcome to AAU Ekpoma Kofa

The Student Information and Registration Portal of
Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma, Nigeria

Kofa is a part of the Africa eUniversity Project (WAeUP).It is a powerful and Comprehensive Student Management System which will become the singe access point to educational resources and information to all AAUE students.

Kofa controls application and student registration processes and provides comprehensive and transparent information about the study progress.

Scam Alert! Ambrose Alli University Disclaimer! The attention of the University has been drawn to an imposter who is using a new Google messaging service available on Google Search Engine to defraud unsuspecting visistors when they search for Ambrose Alli University website on the internet using their laptop computers and Android phones.This messaging service is currently being operated by a yet to be identified fraudster. He/She poses as support staff for payments on the University alumni and undergraduate portals. You are hereby advised not to initiate any conversation with this individual, as this service is not managed by the University or any of her authorized technology vendors. Please be warned!
